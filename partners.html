Convening Partners

The Forum is convened by the World Bank Group with support from ESMAP and a coalition of international partners bringing complementary expertise across renewable energy integration, grid modernization, regional power trade, and industrial decarbonization.

World Bank Group

  • EAP Energy, the practice convening the Forum
  • ESMAP, Energy Sector Management Assistance Program
  • IFC, International Finance Corporation
  • MIGA, Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency
  • World Bank Academy, supporting the knowledge agenda

International Partners

  • International Energy Agency (IEA), Southeast Asia VRE readiness, cross-border interconnection financing
  • International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA), global perspectives on scaling renewables
  • Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, industrial decarbonization, energy–industry synergy, ESCO and platform financing
  • International Solar Alliance (ISA), ministerial roundtable moderation
  • ASEAN Centre for Energy (ACE), ASEAN Power Grid & market integration
  • Asian Development Bank (ADB), APG investment acceleration

Featured Utility & Industry Contributors

  • PLN (Indonesia), ISLE program, smart-grid roadmap, last-mile electrification, finance
  • KEPCO (Korea), digital utility benchmark
  • KPX (Korea Power Exchange), system operations
  • NSMO (Viet Nam), transmission digitalization
  • Tata Power Delhi, distribution-utility digital turnaround
  • CESI, APG subsea cable financing
  • Huaxia Bank, industrial-park decarbonization finance
  • Monsoon Wind, cross-border RE private capital (Lao PDR–Viet Nam)
  • Energy Innovation, industrial decarbonization policy

Government Participation

Ministers and senior officials from Indonesia, the Republic of the Marshall Islands, Federated States of Micronesia, Tuvalu, Papua New Guinea, Fiji, China, the Philippines, Lao PDR and Viet Nam will participate in plenary and parallel sessions.
