Draft Agenda

June 3–5, 2026 · Sofitel Nusa Dua, Bali, Indonesia

The Forum unfolds over three days, opening with high-level ministerial dialogue, followed by parallel technical sessions, and closing with a cultural tour of the Bukit peninsula. The agenda below is a working draft and remains subject to change.

Day 3: “Majesty of the Bukit” Cultural Tour

Friday, June 5, 2026 · Optional cultural program

07:30
Departure from Sofitel Nusa Dua
An early start to beat the heat and Uluwatu crowds.
08:00 – 09:15
Uluwatu Temple

Explore the clifftop temple. Best time for photography as morning light hits the Indian Ocean cliffs. Observe the architecture and resident macaques. uluwatutemple.id

09:15 – 09:45
Transit to GWK Cultural Park

Approximately 20–30 minutes by coach.

09:45 – 11:15
GWK Cultural Park

Focused tour of the Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue, the world’s fourth tallest. Recommended “VIP Buggy Tour” between the Plaza Wisnu, the Lotus Pond, and the statue base. gwkbali.com

11:15 – 11:45
Cultural Minute

15-minute traditional Balinese dance at the GWK Amphitheater, or a visit to the internal gallery.

11:45 – 12:00
Return to Sofitel Nusa Dua

Direct transit back in time for lunch.
