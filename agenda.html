Draft Agenda

June 3–5, 2026 · Sofitel Nusa Dua, Bali, Indonesia

The Forum unfolds over three days, opening with high-level ministerial dialogue, followed by parallel technical sessions, and closing with a cultural tour of the Bukit peninsula. The agenda below is a working draft and remains subject to change.
